# User Personas

## 1. The Bulk Buyer / Surplus Seller
| Attribute | Detail |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Name** | Priya |
| **Age** | 34 |
| **Occupation** | Homemaker |
| **City** | Hyderabad |
| **Income Range** | ₹8L - ₹12L (Household) |
| **Tech Comfort** | 3/5 - Comfortable with WhatsApp, Swiggy, and basic e-commerce, but avoids complex apps. |

**Daily Routine Summary:** Manages household chores, shops for groceries in bulk on weekends to save money, often realizes halfway through the week that fresh produce or large packets won't be consumed before spoiling.
**Goals:**
- Recover some cost from surplus purchases instead of throwing them away.
- Avoid the guilt of food waste.
- Connect with neighbors safely.
**Frustrations:**
- Throwing away perfectly good food/supplies.
- The hassle of coordinating with strangers on Facebook groups (endless messaging, no-shows).
- Safety concerns about inviting strangers to the house.
**How XtraSwap Solves It:** Allows Priya to snap a photo of her surplus, list it in seconds, and have a verified neighbor pick it up with a secure OTP exchange.
**Quote:** *"I hate throwing away good food just because I bought the family pack. If a neighbor can use it, I'd rather sell it cheap."*
**Preferred Device & Time:** Mobile phone; mostly active mid-morning or early evening.
**Feature Priorities:** 
1. Fast listing creation (camera integration).
2. Privacy controls (hiding exact address until order is confirmed).
3. Chat feature to coordinate pickups.

**Feature Priorities:**

- Avoid long-distance drops that take her out of her preferred zone.

**Frustrations:**
- Unpredictable wait times at restaurants on other apps.
- Customers not being available at the drop location.
- Poor routing leading to wasted fuel.
**How XtraSwap Solves It:** XtraSwap offers hyper-local (under 3km) deliveries. Sneha picks up from a neighbor and drops to another neighbor quickly, maximizing her volume of trips per hour.
**Quote:** *"I prefer short trips around my own area. I can do 5 quick neighborhood deliveries in the time it takes to do one long cross-city drop."*
**Preferred Device & Time:** Mobile phone (mounted on bike); active mostly evenings and weekends.
**Feature Priorities:**
1. Clear earnings and distance display before accepting a job.
2. Accurate in-app map routing.
3. OTP verification to prevent "item not delivered" disputes.

## 2-Person Team Note

**MVP Priorities based on Personas:**
- **Critical Personas for Launch:** Priya (Supply) and Arjun (Demand). A marketplace is dead without liquidity. We must focus heavily on making the listing process frictionless for Priya, and the discovery/pickup process exciting for Arjun.
- **Phase 2 Personas:** Sneha (Delivery) and Ravi (B2B). Delivery logistics add significant complexity. For the MVP, emphasize **Self-Pickup** to get the core transaction loop working before introducing the delivery agent flow.
- **Top Feature Focus:** OTP-secured self-pickup, fast camera-integrated listing form, and reliable real-time chat.
